Branding Help

Give the business a name, voice, and first impression.

We help founders turn a loose idea into a clear identity people can recognize, remember, and talk about.

What this can include

Branding does not have to mean a giant agency package. For a cottage business, it can start with a few practical pieces that make the work feel real.

  • Business name ideas and simple name checks.
  • A one-sentence offer people can understand quickly.
  • Starter logo direction, color palette, and type choices.
  • Taglines, short bios, and words for a website or social profile.
  • A tiny brand guide so the founder knows what to use next.

1. Listen

We learn what the founder makes, who they want to serve, and what feeling the business should carry.

2. Shape

We narrow the name, message, visuals, and first customer promise into something clear.

3. Package

We prepare simple files and words the founder can use on a website, flyer, label, or profile.
