About Us

Purpose and practical tools for small founders.

The Cottage Business Lab exists for people who have something worth sharing but do not yet have the language, design, confidence, or community to bring it forward.

Our mission

We help early founders, low-income entrepreneurs, older adults, young hobbyists, and makers build the first real version of their business with care and clarity.

No matter your age, education, or background, we meet you where you are and help you build from there.

Our model

We do more than offer advice. Our organization provides hands-on, in-house support. We build fully functioning websites for our clients, design merchandise and branded materials, and assist with obtaining any necessary certifications.

The Lab is designed as a nonprofit on a pay-what-you-can basis. Our services are available at no required cost to founders, so there are no barriers to support, tools, or networks.

The Virtual Farmers Market creates another layer of support by letting community members champion specific small businesses through mentorship, donations, purchases, or visibility.

FAQ

Common questions

Is this only for people with a registered business?

No. The Lab is especially useful before someone has a formal business. A hobby, recipe, service idea, or community need is enough to begin.

What does pay-what-you-can mean?

Founders choose a realistic contribution when they are able. Sponsors and donors can also help cover project costs for founders who need free support.

Can supporters help without donating money?

Yes. Product photography, supplies, referrals, social sharing, mentorship, and first purchases can all help a founder move forward.

What is the first step?

After filling out the Start Your Journey form, we will contact you to schedule your first consultation. This will be a short 30-minute Zoom session designed to help you better understand what our organization offers, while giving us the chance to learn more about your idea, venture, or business. From there, we can identify the best ways to support you and help bring your vision to life.
