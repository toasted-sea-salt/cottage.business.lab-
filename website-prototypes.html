Website Prototypes

A basic website a founder can actually launch.

We create simple, mobile-friendly websites that explain what the business offers, show how to contact or buy, and give the founder a real place to send people.

What a prototype means here

Prototype does not mean fake. It means the first practical version: small enough to finish, clear enough to share, and flexible enough to improve after real feedback.

  • A homepage or simple multi-section site.
  • Business story, offer, photos, pricing cues, and contact links.
  • Google Form, email, or phone-based inquiry flow.
  • Mobile-friendly layout and accessibility basics.
  • Launch support for a static host or simple website platform.

Plan

We decide what the site needs to say first: what it is, who it is for, and what someone should do next.

Build

We assemble the page, write starter copy, organize photos, and create clear calls to action.

Launch

We help publish the basic site and leave the founder with a list of updates to make next.
